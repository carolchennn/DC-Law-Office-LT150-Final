A GUIDE TO OUR METHODOLOGY
Firms were asked to report a head count of full-time-employee lawyers in each of their Washington metro area offices as of Dec. 31, 2013. The Washington metro area includes the District of Columbia; the Maryland and Virginia counties of Fairfax, Arlington, Prince Georges and Montgomery; and the independent cities of Arlington, Alexandria and Falls Church in Virginia. Some firms chose not to submit data, and in those cases The National Law Journal uses estimates or reporting from other ALM census surveys. An n/a indicates that a firm did not exist prior to 2013; in Novak Druce Connolly Bove + Quiggs case, two firms merged on Jan. 1 last year. A hyphen indicates that the firms partnership data were unavailable. Total partner counts, which were used to break ties, include both equity and nonequity partners. Some head counts have been rounded to whole numbers.
